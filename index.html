I've entered this program with a wealth of experience in both Electrical and Automotive Engineering at York University and Ontario Tech University respectively. Though I did not complete either of those programs, I was able to accomplish notable projects in my time there.

I have maintained a YouTube channel over the last eight years. Though I do not upload on it frequently, it is a creative output for my projects.
